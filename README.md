**WordSprint is a dual-purpose application designed for word lovers, writers, and students.** It combines a fast-paced, brain-teasing word game with a sophisticated, AI-powered "Editor's Report" to analyze any text you provide.

Whether you want to test your lexical limits under pressure or get a professional-grade breakdown of your writing, WordSprint has you covered.

## ‚ú® Key Features

### 1. The WordSprint Game
*   **Dynamic & Fast-Paced Gameplay:** Race against the clock to form as many words as you can from a jumble of letters. It's an exhilarating test of your vocabulary and mental agility.
*   **Intelligent Word Puzzles:** The game's puzzles are generated by an advanced AI (`llama3-8b-8192` via Groq), ensuring a unique and challenging experience every single time.
*   **Offline First:** The core word game runs entirely on your device, allowing you to play anytime, anywhere, without needing an internet connection.

### 2. The AI Editor's Report
*   **Comprehensive Text Analysis:** Go beyond the game with our linguistic analysis feature. Write or paste any text to get an instant report on:
    *   **Sentiment Analysis:** Understand the emotional leaning of your text with a score and label (Positive, Negative, etc.).
    *   **Tone Detection:** Identify primary tones like *Formal, Optimistic, or Confident* to see how your writing comes across.
    *   **Grammatical Corrections:** Get detailed suggestions for spelling, punctuation, and phrasing to improve your work.
    *   **Key Entity Recognition:** Automatically identify and list the important people, places, and concepts in your text.

## üõ†Ô∏è Tech Stack & Architecture

WordSprint is a showcase of modern Android development, built with a focus on performance, scalability, and maintainability.

*   **UI:** 100% [Kotlin](https://kotlinlang.org/) with [Jetpack Compose](https://developer.android.com/jetpack/compose) for a declarative and modern UI.
*   **Architecture:** Clean Architecture principles with a reactive MVVM (Model-View-ViewModel) pattern.
*   **Asynchronous Operations:** [Kotlin Coroutines](https://kotlinlang.org/docs/coroutines-overview.html) and [Flow](https://kotlinlang.org/docs/flow.html) for managing background tasks and data streams.
*   **Dependency Injection:** [Hilt](https://dagger.dev/hilt/) for robust, compile-time-safe dependency management.
*   **Navigation:** [Jetpack Navigation for Compose](https://developer.android.com/jetpack/compose/navigation) to handle all in-app screen transitions.
*   **AI & Networking:** [Retrofit](https://square.github.io/retrofit/) and [OkHttp](https://square.github.io/okhttp/) for communication for puzzle generation and text analysis.
*   **Data Serialization:** [Kotlinx Serialization](https://github.com/Kotlin/kotlinx.serialization) for parsing JSON data from the API.
*   **Local Storage:** [Jetpack DataStore](https://developer.android.com/topic/libraries/architecture/datastore) for storing user preferences and high scores.
*   **Image Loading:** [Coil](https://coil-kt.github.io/coil/) for efficient and simple image loading in Compose.
